Bent over row mistakes and corrections tips. This video shows the correct way to perform a barbell bent over row. ❌ Do not pull the weights diagonally. This doesn't work against the line of resistance. ❌ Do not stand upright. This limits the range of motion. ❌ Do not use momentum. This stresses the lower back and doesn't effectively train the muscles. ✅ Pull the weights vertically up to work against the line of resistance (any free weight provides resistance in a vertical line due to gravity). ✅ Maintain the torso parallel to the ground to allow a maximum range of motion. #bentoverrow  #bentoverrows  #barbellrows  #workout  #gymtips  #workouttips  #gym
